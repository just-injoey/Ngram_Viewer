1. 
A CONFESSION1 
[1884] 
I wrote it on a slip of paper and handed it to him myself. In 
this note not only did I confess my guilt, but I asked adequate 
punishment for it, and closed with a request to him not to punish 
himself for my offence. 
I also pledged myself never to steal in 
future. 2 
An Autobiography, Pt. I, Ch. VIII
